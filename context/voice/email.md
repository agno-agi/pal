## Structure

- Subject line style: [tone guidance]
- Salutation: [style]
- Ask section: first or last
- Body: concise paragraphs, one purpose per paragraph
- Close: clear next step + deadline if applicable

## Anti-Patterns

- Banned words: [overly pushy, vague politeness phrases]
- Banned openings: ["Just circling back" if not in context, etc.]
- Formatting to avoid: [no dense blocks, no unclear action items]

## Platform Conventions

- Tone should reflect relationship and urgency context.
- Prefer scannable bullets for tasks and deadlines.
- Keep action items explicit with owners and dates.

## Examples

### Good

- "Hi [Name],\n\nQuick update: I reviewed the draft and shared edits in the attached notes.\n\nCould you confirm by [date] so we can finalize?"
- "Thanks for moving this forward. I confirmed timing and attached the proposed next steps."

### Bad

- "I hope you’re doing very well. I wanted to share my thoughts.\n\nWe appreciate your kind cooperation and would be grateful for any progress at your convenience."
- "Please do review and revert at your earliest convenience at your earliest convenience."
